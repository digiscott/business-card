## Full page content

# Supporting Better Conversations in the Exam Room

PatientPoint's exam room experience is part of its point-of-care platform, helping providers educate patients and support more meaningful conversations during the visit. The product context includes interactive education, relevant health content, and tools that help patients better understand conditions, procedures, and treatment options.

During my time at PatientPoint, I contributed to the product and platform work supporting this type of exam room experience. The challenge was not only delivering content to screens, but making sure the right educational material could be created, organized, deployed, and maintained across a complex healthcare content ecosystem.

My work focused on connecting product needs, editorial workflows, engineering implementation, and stakeholder expectations. I helped translate needs from clinical, creative, product, and technical teams into clearer requirements, workflows, and implementation paths.

## The problem

Exam room education needs to be timely, accurate, specialty-relevant, and easy for providers to use during real conversations with patients.

## My contribution

I helped bridge the gap between content strategy, technical systems, and product execution by supporting requirements, workflows, and internal tooling connected to PatientPoint's exam room experience.

## Product impact

This work supported a more scalable content and platform foundation for patient education experiences used at the point of care.

## What this shows about my work

I can operate across design, content, product, and engineering to help turn complex healthcare workflows into usable product experiences.

# Turning Practice Communication Into a Product Experience

PatientPoint's Practice Engagement positioning focuses on helping healthcare practices communicate more effectively with staff, support practice initiatives, and create more consistent visibility across care teams.

The product challenge was broader than displaying messages on a screen. Practices needed reliable ways to manage communication, support staff engagement, and keep content relevant across varied healthcare environments.

My work on PracticeWire / Practice Engagement involved helping connect business goals, customer needs, content operations, and platform capabilities. I supported the translation of stakeholder needs into product requirements and helped clarify how internal teams could manage, deploy, and support the experience over time.

## The problem

Healthcare practices need simple, visible, repeatable ways to communicate with staff without relying only on email, meetings, or fragmented tools.

## My contribution

I helped shape requirements and workflows that supported practice-facing communication experiences, while connecting stakeholder goals to the systems and teams responsible for delivering them.

## Product impact

The work supported PatientPoint's broader effort to make practice communication more consistent, visible, and actionable through digital engagement technology.

## What this shows about my work

I'm strong at turning operational complexity into product structure, especially when multiple internal teams, users, and systems need to align.

## Full page content

# Making Patient Engagement More Relevant

PatientPoint's life sciences positioning includes precise patient targeting across trusted point-of-care channels, including check-in, patient portal, waiting room, exam room, staff meeting room, mobile, and programmatic experiences.

For product teams, this type of work creates a complex challenge: the experience must connect data, content, compliance, targeting logic, customer needs, and patient-facing communication channels.

My contribution was in helping think through the product and workflow implications of delivering more targeted, relevant healthcare content. This included understanding how content systems, metadata, internal tools, and delivery platforms needed to support more precise patient engagement.

## The problem

Healthcare content becomes more valuable when it reaches the right audience in the right context, but that requires strong connections between data, content, targeting, and delivery systems.

## My contribution

I helped connect product requirements, content operations, and platform capabilities so teams could better understand what was needed to support targeted patient engagement workflows.

## Product impact

This work supported the larger PatientPoint strategy of moving from broad content distribution toward more relevant, data-informed patient engagement.

## What this shows about my work

I can help teams reason through emerging product opportunities where business strategy, data, user needs, and technical systems all overlap.
